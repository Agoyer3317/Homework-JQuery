Puppy Collector

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.There are four puppies below. By clicking on a puppy you will add a specific amount to your total score. You will win the game my matching your total score to random number, you lose the game if your total sore goes above the Random Number. The value of each puppy is hidden from you until you click on it. Each time when the game starts, the game will change the value of each puppy.
